What is the hydro jetting process like, and will it damage my pipes?
Hydro jetting uses high-pressure water to remove grease, debris, and scale from your pipes. We begin by inspecting your plumbing with a video camera to assess the blockage and pipe condition. Our technicians adjust the water pressure to match your pipe material—whether older cast iron or newer PVC—to prevent damage. Hydro jetting is generally safe for most pipes when performed by professionals, and can actually extend the life of your plumbing by thoroughly cleaning it.
