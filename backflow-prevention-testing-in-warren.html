Frequently Asked Questions About Backflow Prevention Testing

How much does backflow prevention testing cost in Warren, MI?

We offer discounts for testing multiple devices at the same property. Pricing includes the inspection, test report submission to the City of Warren, and a copy for your records. If repairs are needed, we’ll provide an upfront estimate before proceeding.
How long does the backflow testing process take and when can you schedule it?
Most backflow prevention device tests take about 30 to 45 minutes per device. We strive to schedule appointments within a week of your call, and often have next-day availability during the spring and summer, which are Warren’s busiest testing seasons. Testing is required annually by the city, so we recommend booking early, especially if your renewal deadline is approaching.
Is DNA Payroll Service licensed and certified for backflow testing in Michigan?
Yes, our plumbers are fully licensed in the State of Michigan and hold ASSE (American Society of Sanitary Engineering) certification for backflow prevention assembly testing and repair. We’re registered with the City of Warren as an approved backflow tester, and our reports are accepted by the city’s Water Division.
What happens if my backflow device fails the test?
If your device does not pass the initial test, we’ll explain the issue in detail and provide a written estimate for the necessary repairs. Most common problems can be fixed on the spot. Once repairs are complete, we’ll retest the device at no additional charge and submit the passing report to the City of Warren to ensure you remain in compliance.
Do you guarantee your backflow testing and repairs?
Absolutely. We stand behind our work with a 1-year warranty on all backflow repairs we perform. If you have any issues with a device we’ve serviced, call us and we’ll address the issue promptly at no additional cost.
